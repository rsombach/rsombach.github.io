We Belong

The We Belong movement is the pursuit and support in communities for meaningful, paid work opportunities for people with intellectual disabilities. The goal of the We Belong app is to reward forward-thinking businesses who hire inclusively by giving consumers a quick, easy place to find those employers. We hope this will inspire others businesses to do the same.
